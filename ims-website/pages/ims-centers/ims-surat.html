About IMS Surat

Surat’s Trusted Coaching for Entrance Exams

IMS Surat has been mentoring ambitious students with focused, outcome-driven entrance exam preparation. Built on the strong IMS legacy since 1977, the centre has supported hundreds of Surat aspirants in progressing toward their academic and career milestones.

With guidance from experienced faculty and committed mentors, IMS Surat delivers a structured learning experience through interactive classroom sessions, well-planned study schedules, realistic mock tests, and continuous performance evaluation.

At IMS Surat, clarity of concepts and consistent practice drive sustainable success.

IMS Surat trains students for a wide range of competitive entrance examinations, including:

  • MBA Entrance Exams: CAT, XAT, NMAT, CMAT
  • Law & Undergraduate Exams: CLAT, AILET, IPMAT, BBA

Along with exam preparation, IMS Surat offers career counselling, profile building, and admission guidance. Students from Surat have earned admissions into IIMs, top NLUs, reputed private institutes, and international universities, converting focused preparation into real-world achievements.
