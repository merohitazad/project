About Us

Gujarat's #1 Offline Coaching Institute

IMS has been preparing students for entrance tests since 1977. Every year, IMS mentors 50,000+ students and helps them secure seats in coveted institutes in India as well as across the world.

IMS’s highly experienced faculty from reputed institutes such as IIMs, XLRI, NMIMS, GNLU, along with well-researched study material, closest possible mock tests, and comprehensive online resources make IMS a top choice.

Our training programs are so superior that they come with a guarantee!

IMS Ahmedabad prepares students for MBA entrance exams such as:

  • CAT, IIFT, XAT, NMAT, CMAT
  • International: GMAT, GRE, IELTS, SAT
  • Undergraduate: CLAT, AILET (Law), Integrated MBA, BBA

Along with exam prep, IMS also provides admit/scholarship guarantees in top 100 global universities. Our students regularly secure admission to NLUs, IIM-I, Symbiosis, NMIMS, NIRMA, PDPU, etc.
